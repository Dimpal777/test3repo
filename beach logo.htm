    Maja Mexican Grill

    Maja Mexican Grill Brand Identity 2016 _______ Legend has it some surfers pray for good waves… others just pray for good food! In Huntington Beach, California, where surf culture reigns supreme, this client wanted a logo for his restaurant that was befitting of the protective community of talented men and women who call the ocean home. Derived from surf lingo, “Maja” meaning mother, is a term of affection often used within this community. The objective was to design a wordmark that represented the free spirit of the bad-asses who meet out in the water each day while also making sense for a professional restaurant establishment. The custom, hand-written design with unpredictable characters and wild movement was born out of the desire to appeal to local surfers as well as anyone else who may have a fiery hunger for authentic Mexican food! This handmade logo also features multiple variations including a version paying homage to the famed Huntington Beach lifeguard towers.

So the general idea from the briefing was to use a pineapple, which takes two years to grow, as a main element in the design to represent that period.
